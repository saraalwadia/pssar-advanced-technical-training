## Reflection

The hardest part of this assignment was merging multiple datasets with different structures and ensuring consistency between them.

I had to carefully clean the funding data by handling null values, negative numbers, and incorrect types.

The analysis revealed:
- A small number of researchers contribute most of the citations.
- Funding is highly concentrated in specific fields.
- The earliest researchers are not always the most active today.

This shows how research data is unevenly distributed and highlights the importance of proper data integration and cleaning.
